ALLERGENS

Belgium is renowned worldwide for its exceptional chocolate, which comes in a wide variety of types and styles, each crafted with precision and tradition. The most famous type is pralines, which are chocolate shells filled with ganache, caramel, or other flavored fillings. Dark chocolate, with its rich, intense flavor, is a staple, made from a high percentage of cocoa, often with a smooth and slightly bitter taste. Milk chocolate is another popular variety, offering a creamier, sweeter profile with a smooth texture. White chocolate, made from cocoa butter without the cocoa solids, is another beloved type, known for its sweet and velvety flavor. Belgium is also famous for artisan chocolates, where small batches are created by skilled chocolatiers, often using local ingredients like fruits, nuts, and spices. Each variety reflects the country's deep history and commitment to chocolate-making excellence.
